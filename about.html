About the Rating Hub

My personal catalog of all things worth watching.

What is This Website?

This is a passion project — a personal hobby website built simply to keep track of every movie, anime, and series I've watched. Think of it as my comprehensive digital viewing diary! The motivation is twofold: to store a full list for my own memory, and to encourage others to discover the great content I've enjoyed.

The Rating Philosophy: Purely Personal

Every score you see here (out of 10) is 100% subjective. It's not based on critical consensus or box office success, but purely on my personal enjoyment, emotional impact, and how much I'd recommend it to a friend. If a movie is rated high, it means I believe it's genuinely worth your time.

  • 10/10 : A personal favorite; a must-watch experience.
  • 8-9/10: Excellent entertainment and highly recommended.
  • 5-7/10: Solid but flawed; good for a casual watch.
  • 1-5/10: Very bad; Not worth your time.

Get Inspired to Watch!

While this is my list, my hope is that you use it as a source of genuine, personalized recommendations. Scroll through the Movies, Anime, and Series sections — if something catches your eye and has a strong rating, give it a shot!

Thanks for visiting my corner of the web. Happy watching!
