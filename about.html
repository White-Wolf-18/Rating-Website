About the Rating Hub

My personal catalog of all things worth watching.

What is This Website?

This is a passion project — a personal hobby website built simply to keep track of every movie, anime, and series I've watched. Think of it as my comprehensive digital viewing diary! The motivation is twofold: to store a full list for my own memory, and to encourage others to discover the great content I've enjoyed.
